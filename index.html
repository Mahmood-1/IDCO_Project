INTRODUCTION

The Improvement and Development for Communities Centre (IDCO) has been successful in attracting a large number of high-value projects in recent years. Many of these projects are innovative and transformational in support of long term strategy. These projects are a welcome sign of our success and our investment for the future. It is vital that these pioneering projects are well managed by proficient Project Managers and by their teams and adequately supported by centre’s administrative support staff. Due to the size and strategic importance of these projects, it is essential that we meet agreed timescales, budgets and levels of performance. Failure to do so can result in serious financial loss and damage to our reputation. Consequently, one of the most important and supported management processes in IDCO must be proficient 'project management'. IDCO has a number of internal departmental project management processes and these have evolved over time to meet the needs of targeted groups, stakeholders or budget holders. Many of these processes have served IDCO well, however the valuable, larger size and complexity of current projects has put a strain on these existing project management systems. Furthermore, the IDCO’s PMS supports the UN-Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

About IDCO?

In early 2014, IDCO was registered as a center of strategic studies, researches and development by the Palestinian Ministry of Information in Ramallah, with its mission to stimulate community's improvement and development as one of the most outstanding careers serving generations and their resources. IDCO are prioritizing interventions and projects for the coming three years into four main categories which form the IDCO’s endorsed strategy as follow:
1. Contribute to the promotion of community reconciliation and unity. 2. Strengthen capacities of the developmental sectors. 3. Effective participation in the community development issues specially for women and Youth. 4. Support the IDCO’s Sustainable Role and Impacts.


IDCO philosophy is adopting a multi-developmental programs aiming at contribute in solving the community's challenges, especially, the challenges related to community development, sustainability, food security, health, advocacy and environment conservation. IDCO will seek to achieve these programs through the implementation of consultancies, studies, researches, activities and projects as well as to stimulate positive community's initiatives.

Mission Statement

Our IDCO believes

Initiation

IDCO encourages employees to freely express their opinions and present their creative ideas

Unity

IDCO believes in the necessity of the group work. The management entirely accepts to carry out the decisions taken by the upper and HQ levels

Transparency

IDCO considers well defined procedures and clear information

Justice

IDCO applies equal standards with all employees and service recipients in a subjective and non-biased way

Integrity and honesty

IDCO members and employees are trustworthy and could be counted on

Teamwork culture

IDCO members and employees cooperate to achieve the goals and respect each other’s opinions

Commitment

IDCO pays efforts to enhance living standards of Palestinians. Dedication from all IDCO staff and volunteers to achieve this goal is mandatory

Independency

IDCO provides a platform through which staff/members could mutually reach an agreement on opinions. It has a non-biased management that helps those members to achieve the goals

Partnership

IDCO belongs to the Palestinian NGO community, promotes for cooperation and coordination in different domains, and establishes relations with other NGOs based on mutual understanding and respect of privacy and independency of each party

Equality

IDCO commits to promote for full and equal participation from all parties (including women) in the development process. The centre commits to create a balance between men and women at work

Diversity

IDCO respects and considers different approaches to achieve results; consequently, the management selects employees from a wide geographical range and regardless of their political views

IDCO MANAGEMENT

The IDCO’s BoD and staff members are from different backgrounds and expertise, are executing successfully the centre’s management tasks and activities including for instance; update and endorsing the internal policies, regulations, approaches and strategies as well as communicate and collaborating with all same-scope stakeholders, donors and the targeted communities in order to develop professionally the frameworks and propositions that responding better to the needs, gapes and concerns of peoples in Palestine. The centre’s BOD and the executing teams are conducting their meeting periodically and whenever needed in order to follow up the centres’ programs and activities and taking the suitable decisions accordingly. Accordingly, IDCO management and staff are adopting a multi-developmental programs aiming at contribute in solving the community's challenges, especially, the challenges related to community development, sustainability, food security, health, advocacy and environment conservation. IDCO will seek to achieve these programs through the implementation of consultancies, studies, researches, activities and projects as well as to stimulate positive community's initiatives. Actually, ToR/job descriptions are available for staff and clearly define roles and responsibilities of employees, and the management of IDCO is systematically review the performance of its employees and engaging them in all areas of decision making in accordance to their responsibilities and roles the organization’s chart. Moreover, IDCO management promote staff learning and development in line with their area of expertise and with the objectives and mandate of the organization and the organization advises employees, beneficiaries, and other recipients to whom to report cases of un-ethical behaviour by staff members, such as harassment, sexual abuse and exploitation, discrimination, etc. On the other hand, IDCO management have developed fraud policy which covers, how to respond to allegations of fraud, how to expect to deter fraud, how to respond to different types of fraud, and how allegations can be reported, and how the allegation will be investigated, all staff are aware and know about this policy.
